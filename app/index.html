Measuring Instructions

Please do your best and remember these measurements do not have to be perfect
It is simply helping you get your rough estimate quicker and budget to speed up this overall process for you.

Windows

  • Please take your tape measure and follow the photo instructions.
  • Place your tape measure on one side of the window trim/casing, and draw it across the width of the window to the opposite side trim/casing. This is your WIDTH.
  • Follow the same steps from top to bottom, and this is your HEIGHT.

Doors

  • Please take your tape measure and follow the photo instructions.
  • Place your tape measure on one side of the door's trim/casing, and draw it across the width of the door to the opposite side trim/casing. This is your WIDTH.
  • Draw your tape measure from the top of the door's trim/casing, and pull it down to the bottom of your floor. This is your HEIGHT.

Outside of the house

  • Please take 1 photo of each elevation of your home (front, left, right, back) with the entire side of the home in camera view. It is optional, but encouraged.
